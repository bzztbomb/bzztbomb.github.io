I'm a cellist with lots of experience in classical music. An interest in modern and avant garde music has gotten me into using electronic/computer technology to make music. I spent a year at Stanford's Center for Computer Research in Music and Acoustics, where I began to learn about microcontrollers and design for human-computer interaction, among other things.

I have written up some of my projects here: http://homepage.mac.com/coldham/klang
