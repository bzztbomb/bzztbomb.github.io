ZAP! POW! Come on down on Wednesday!

By: breedx

2007-12-03 23:11:12

Portland Dorkbot will be having an informal gathering on Wednesday, December 5th at 7:30pm at Vendetta on N Williams. Come on down and bring your mom. We're generally pretty easy to spot. Be on the lookout for out-of-place uncomfortable dorks with glowing laptop screens and piles of gadgetry on the tables. Amen. Although this is an informal gathering, we always encourage people to bring their latest hacks to show off, work on, or just basically share with the group. There have been some very thought provoking and creative discussions lately, and things are heating up for the beginning of 2008...so come on!
