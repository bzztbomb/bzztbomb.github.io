nerdkits projects have some neat ideas. 2009-02-13 21:18:59 PhilipOfOregon

the NerdKits website has some interesting projects and tutorials.

It's a bit lower-level than Arduino projects, but it looks like they've got significant helpful detail. In addition, they've got tutorials which show how to interface external computers (IPhone, web servers) to your Arduino.

I learned something helpful from their "How to Strip Wire" and "Valentine's Day LED Heart" tutorials.
