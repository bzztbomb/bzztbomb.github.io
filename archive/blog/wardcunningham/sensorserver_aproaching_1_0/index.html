SensorServer aproaching 1.0

By: WardCunningham

2010-02-27 18:25:36

After a year of occasional fiddling I am finally happy with the stability and convenience of my sensor network. Some features include:

  • Arduino only configuration with off-premise data recording
  • Analog, digital, bynase and 1-wire support with scaling and calibration
  • Device discovery, labels, documentation and tag-based grouping
  • Interactive graphs at time scales from hours to years with decimation and missing-data support

For your browsing pleasure I offer my latest sensor addition, simple ac line voltage monitoring, or browse my whole network.
