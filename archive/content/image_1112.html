ByteBeats Clean w/ Teensy-3.1 2014-05-26 20:59:01 spacewrench

I built a version of the ByteBeats circuit with a Teensy-3.1 I had on hand.  It works the same as the Teensy-2.0 versions, but it probably has a higher top frequency.  (Doesn't matter, I can't hear that high anyway.)

I also updated the program to be able to play regular notes instead of the crazy one-line-of-code melodies that we used during the workshop.

Thanks to everybody who participated; I hope you all had fun & learned something.  Don't forget, you can use the same circuit to do a lot of other audio things as well.
