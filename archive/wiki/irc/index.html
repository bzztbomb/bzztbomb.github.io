IRC

By: tlockney

2007-05-05 00:04:00

Come chat with dorks via IRC on the FreeNode network:

Server: chat.freenode.net
Channel: #dorkbotpdx

We're live 24/7. Sometimes. Swing on by (now!) and say "hi".

If you're not already an IRC user, here's some reading for you:

  • http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IRC
  • http://freenode.net/using_the_network.shtml

For possible clients, it really depends on your OS of choice.

For Unix-like systems, Irssi and BitchX seem to be the most popular, but Pidgin also supports IRC:

  • http://www.irssi.org/
  • http://www.bitchx.com/
  • http://pidgin.im/

For OSX, it would seem everyone likes Colloquy:

  • http://colloquy.info/
  • Textual: https://www.codeux.com/textual/

For Windows, mIRC is almost ubiquitous:

  • http://www.mirc.com/

(but you really should fdisk and install a better OS)
