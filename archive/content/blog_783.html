Smallest Federated Wiki 2011-08-13 21:08:50 WardCunningham A month or so I suggested what I thought would be a one-day project. I asked my colleagues at the Indie Web Camp to create the Smallest Federated Wiki. The idea was that wiki would be simpler if I kept my pages and you kept yours. Somehow we'd share pages through some sort of "refactoring" browser. Well, the prototype is working and showing well. I invite you to watch the 2-4 minute screencasts I've posted on my new Wiki Channel on vimeo. Here is the introduction.
