Background

What is circuit bending?

Where did it come from? - Reed's "Alien" philosophy - Anti-theory

How does this differ from "modding"? - Philosophy vs. experience - Aleatoric/chance elements...sometimes it won't work, sometimes it won't be repeatable

Show examples of bent instruments

  • Famous Speak and Spells
  • Incantor
  • SK1

Types of things to bend

Battery powered, respect the electron

  • Kids toys
  • Guitar/effects pedals
  • Halloween props
  • Keyboards

Stay away from (unless you're daring/risky):

  • TVs, microwaves, cars, other deadly items
  • Raw power and stored charge

General classes of bends

  • Line out
  • Feedback/squeal
  • Auto loop points
  • Pitch/clock change
  • Glitch
  • Radio/noise/static
  • Combinations of all of the above
  • Something entirely weird and new!

Techniques

  • Line out (watch the level, trimmed + potted level)
    • First thing to do, changes sound completely
  • Feel around with hands
    • Often provides general regions of interest - Can be fine-tuned later - Good source of touch/contact points
  • Single point to point shorts (become switches)
    • jeweler's screwdrivers or wire and alligator clips
    • watch out for short to ground/short to power
  • Pot/capacitor/resistor/leds (become switched components)
  • Potted power
  • New bus (Reed's X to X)
  • Crashes
    • Switched power for reset
  • Finishing
    • Recasing or not

Tips

  • Scratching at traces
    • Can be helpful where no pin/solder joint available (but fragile)
  • Always record initial probes/searches
  • Take pictures, scribble notes with software
    • Can help others too
  • Stranded wire versus solid core
  • Saving battery holders/switches/LEDs
    • Also converting coin cell to AA packs
