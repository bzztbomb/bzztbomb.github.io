Single Sided PCBs 2008-11-12 18:09:58 Laen In preparation for making PCBs, I've created some little one-sided PCBs in Eagle. They are: * Moto - An L293D H-Bridge breakout board. * Tiny45-USB-SMD - A tiny45 and the circuitry necessary to talk USB. * MagicHat Controller - An Atmega168 based board with an i2c header for driving 12 vibe motors.
