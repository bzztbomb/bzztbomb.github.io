DorkbotPDX is pround to announce its PCB Group Ordering service! Now you can have your own printed circuit boards made for $2.50 per square inch. No minimums, no setup fees.

We intend to provide this as a monthly service to start with. If it's popular enough, we may make it a bi-monthly service like our Parts group ordering.

Details inside.

Why would you want this?

  • You get beautiful, green, professional looking boards.
  • It beats huffing chlorine gas while etching your boards at home.
  • All boards will be produced here in the US of A

How this works

Submit your board as an Eagle .brd file or a set of gerber files, and one of our Gerber Babies will merge them all together into a large panel and ship it off for production.

Boards will be distributed at the following meeting.

Limitations and specs

  • Minimum trace width is 8 mils.
  • Minimum distance between traces is 8 mils.
  • Minimum drill size is 20 mils.
  • 1-2 layer boards only
