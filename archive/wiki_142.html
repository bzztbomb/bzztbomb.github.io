DorkbotPDX is open to everyone. If you have an interest in things electronic and possibly even the slightest leaning towards things artistic, you belong with us. See the meetings page for information about when we are holding our next meeting and come out and join us.

Feel free to add a page for yourself to this site describing your interests, hobbies, or whatever. Also, keep in mind that not all of our members are represented here. Also keep in mind that there is no clear definition of what constitutes membership in DorkbotPDX, other than participating as part of our community. We also ask that you not link outside of the dorkbot community here. If you want to provide a link to your own site, create a wiki page for yourself, describing who you are and what you do and add the link there.
