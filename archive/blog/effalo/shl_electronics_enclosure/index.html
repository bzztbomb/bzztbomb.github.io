shL is an enclosure framework for rapid interaction prototyping. it is compatible with payloads that can fit within a 4.5" x 4.5" x 1.5" space. it is made of translucent plastic which is easy to modify and nicely diffuses LED light. great for arduinos, other microcontrollers, or electronics projects. made of locally sourced found materials. more information here: http://envvv.com/shl
